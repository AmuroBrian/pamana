ABOUT
The website, meticulously crafted by a talented team of developers including Jerome Del Castillo, Cristian John Norono, Julien Ibalio, and Marc Drei under the visionary leadership of Brian Perez, stands as a testament to their dedication and expertise. Commissioned by the esteemed RPH subject instructor, Mr. Rafael Riesgo, the website beautifully explores the rich tapestry of Filipino culture across various historical epochs, spanning from the pre-colonial era to the modern day. With a thematic focus on "FILIPINO CULTURE PRE COLONIAL SPANISH JAPANESE AMERICAN TO MODERN DAY PERIOD," the site serves as a digital repository, presenting the intricate heritage of the Filipino people. Users can embark on a captivating journey through time, delving into the customs, traditions, and societal nuances that shaped the Filipino identity. Moreover, the website adeptly illustrates how the modern-day period is intricately woven with the threads of its historical predecessors, offering a nuanced perspective on the evolution of Filipino culture across diverse influences. Imbued with meticulous design and insightful content, this digital platform serves as a virtual gateway to the heart and soul of the Philippines, providing an engaging and educational experience for visitors eager to explore the fascinating facets of Filipino heritage.
